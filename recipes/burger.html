Burger Recipe

Description

A burger is a classic favorite! While some recipes can be complicated,
the one shared here is simple and easy to follow.

Mmm... burgers are just so good. Mmmmmm...

Ingredients

  • A burger bun
  • Beef patty
  • Cheese
  • Bacon
  • Iceberg lettuce
  • condiments like:
    • mayonnaise
    • ketchup

Steps

  1. Place the bottom bun on a plate
  2. Add a layer of lettuce
  3. Place the beef patty on top
  4. Add a slice of cheese
  5. Add the bacon
  6. Top with your favorite condiments
  7. Finish with the top bun
  8. Enjoy your burger!
