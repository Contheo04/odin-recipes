Sandwich Recipe

Description

A sandwich is a classic and tasty option! While some recipes can be complicated,
this one is easy to follow and delicious.

Mmm... sandwiches are just so good. Mmmmmm...

Ingredients

  • Bread loaf
  • Cheese
  • Bacon
  • Iceberg lettuce
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Toast or warm up the bread
  2. Layer the ingredients to build your sandwich
  3. Add condiments of your choice (we recommend mayonnaise!)
  4. Enjoy your sandwich!
